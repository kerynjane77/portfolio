Front end web developer, visual designer and crazy diamond. I love good design, typography, clean code, art and dark chocolate.

About me

As a long time print graphic designer I knew I had to master the digital space. Teaching myself online didn’t work, so I turned to General Assembly’s Web Development Immersive course.

At GA I’ve learned the basics of web development, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Ruby, Sinatra, Rails, APIs, Git/Github, and PostgreSQL. My graphic design experience means I’m fluent in Adobe creative suite, developing design concepts, and working in a team to find good design solutions.

My design and art background, combined with my tech skills, helps me create beautiful and easy to use websites and apps, which integrate technology and aesthetics.

Now I’ve started my journey to become an expert front end developer, and I’m excited to put my web dev skills to use on real life projects, and continue learning.
